[Hook]
I feel your shadow moving closer in the night
A phantom whisper making everything feel right
The rain is falling softly on the window pane
But all I want is just to taste your lips again

[Verse 1]
The timber crackles and the shadows start to play
As every weary trouble gently melts away
Your breath is warm against the chill of midnight air
My fingers tracing every secret hidden there

[Pre-Chorus]
The silence speaks in rhythms only we can know
A steady river pulling me into the flow
We're caught up in the magic of a sudden spark
Igniting fires waiting deep inside the dark

[Chorus]

Are soaking through my soul and washing out the pain (All the pain)
I need the shelter of your body close to mine
To let our tangled shadows slowly intertwine

[Post-Chorus]
